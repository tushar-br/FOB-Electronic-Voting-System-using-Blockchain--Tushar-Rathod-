How It Works

A transparent and secure process powered by blockchain technology.

1. Cast Vote

User logs in securely and selects their preferred candidate. The vote is encrypted immediately.

2. Block Creation

The encrypted vote is bundled into a new block. A unique cryptographic hash is generated.

3. Chain Addition

The new block is linked to the previous block, creating an immutable chain that cannot be altered.
